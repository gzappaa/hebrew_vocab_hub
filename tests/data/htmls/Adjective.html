Inflection of אֲבִיבִי https://www.pealim.com

Adjective

Root: א - ב - ב

The first radical of this word is guttural; this affects the adjacent vowels.

Derived from אָבִיב spring (season) and ־ִי.

Meaning

spring-like, vernal
